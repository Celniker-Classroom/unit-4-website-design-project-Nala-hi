Sunny-side up egg recipe

Sunny-side-up eggs are eggs only cooked on one side, leaving the yolk intact and runny. The dish is said to resemble the shining sun.

Ingredients/equipment

  • butter
  • 1 fresh egg
  • seasoning of choice
  • nonstick pan

Instructions

  1. Heat up and distribute butter on the pan on med-high heat for a couple minutes. Crack a large egg into a separate bowl.

  2. Slide the egg into the pan on med-low heat and immediate cover with a lid. Cook without disturbing the eggs until the whites are set (2-3 minutes).

  3. Slide the eggs out the pan, season with salt and ground pepper, and serve.
