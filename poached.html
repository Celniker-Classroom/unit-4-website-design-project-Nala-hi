Poached Egg Recipe

Poached eggs are delicately cooked to achieve firm whites and a runny yolk. I promise this healthy, fat-free method of cooking eggs is quick and easy after getting the hang of the technique. A poached egg is perfect to eat by itself or in addition to toast, egg benedicts, salad, and other dishes, to spice up your breakfast. Yum!

Ingredients/equipment

  • butter
  • fresh eggs
  • seasoning of choice
  • nostick pan

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil before turning the heat back to low.
  2. Crack an egg into a mesh strainer to strain out the liquidy part of the white, and place the rest of the egg in a bowl.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of vinegar into the water and stir to create a vortex
  4. Quickly and gently slide the egg into the vortex. Cook for three minutes before taking the egg out with a ladle
  5. Pat the egg dry with a paper towel and serve.
